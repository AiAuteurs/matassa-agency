The Name

A Sicilian surname rooted in the textile trades.
The name traces to Castelbuono, Sicily.

Literal

A skein — a length of thread, wool, rope, or wire, wound into a loose coil. Ready to be used. Ready to be pulled into something whole.

Figurative

A tangled situation. A complex problem. Cercare il bandolo della matassa — to find the key to a problem. Sbrogliare la matassa — to disentangle what's complicated.

The Connection
