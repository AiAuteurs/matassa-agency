The Name MATASSA

From Castelbuono, Sicily.

In Italian, matassa means skein —
a length of thread, wound and
ready to be pulled into
something whole.

New York · Los Angeles
San Francisco · Atlanta · Bangkok

That's what I do.
I take scattered pieces — footage, ideas, moments — and pull the right threads to shape a story that works.

I've been doing that for 30+ years as a film editor, crafting TV spots, sizzles, pitches, and short-form content for agencies, brands, and startups. I've worked alongside some of the best in the business — from Jeff Goodby to Dennis Virkler to Michael Bay — and that foundation still drives everything I do.

Today, that craft extends into cinematic A.I.

For the past three years, I've been working at the intersection of traditional filmmaking and A.I. — collaborating with leading platforms and pushing what's possible in modern storytelling. That work has been recognized at international festivals, including Cannes and Bali, and as a finalist in the Alibaba Thailand Film Festival and Runway Gen:48.

But tools don't make the work.
Story does. Taste does.
Experience does.

When you work with Matassa, you're not just getting visuals — you're getting judgment. Structure. Pacing. Ideas that elevate the work. A partner who hits deadlines, communicates clearly, and protects your budget.

From first idea to final cut, I help you make something that lands.

Whether it's a TV spot, a pitch, a case study, or a film —
I'm here to help you win.
