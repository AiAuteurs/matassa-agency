Matassa Agency is built on a simple premise: the world's most advanced AI production tools are only as good as the creative instincts behind them.

Michael Matassa has spent 36 years in the edit suite with Michael Bay, Disney, BBDO, Goodby Silverstein, and the world's most demanding creative directors. He knows what makes a frame land. He knows what makes a viewer feel something.

Over the past several years, he has gone deep on generative AI filmmaking — integrating emerging tools into real-world professional workflows as a Creative Partner with ByteDance Dreamina, Runway, and Alibaba WAN AI, working hands-on with tools before public release.

This isn't AI doing the work alone. This is 36 years of taste, wielding tomorrow's tools. And it's available to your marketing team, on retainer, on demand.
